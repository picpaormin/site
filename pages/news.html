Member's Amnesty and Reinstatement Programs

Board Resolution No. 2021-07-24

The Member's Amnesty and Reinstatement Programs were approved by the National Board of Directors in their Board meeting last July 24, 2021.

The Amnesty Program will allow members with delinquent accounts to become members in good standing by paying only the current year's membership dues of P1,000.00 up to December 31, 2021.

Further, members with delinquent accounts failing to avail of the above Member's Amnesty Program, may avail of the Member's Reinstatement Program by paying a reinstatement fee of P1,000.00 on top of the current year's membership dues of P1,000.00 up to June 30, 2021.

The Amnesty Program is effective July 24, 2021, and the Reinstatement Program will be effective January 01, 2022.
